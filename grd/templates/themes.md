<guidelines>

**Theme quality:** A theme captures a meaningful pattern in the data, not just a recurring word or topic. Each theme should tell a story that contributes to answering the research questions.

**Citation format:** All evidence references use `[Note: {note-name}]` inline format. Every claim must be traceable to a specific research note.

**Coverage requirement:** Every verified research note must appear in at least one theme's supporting evidence. Orphan notes (notes that appear in no theme) must be documented in Unaddressed Areas with an explanation.

**Contradictions:** Surface conflicting evidence explicitly. Do not average away disagreements between notes. Contradictions are analytically valuable.

</guidelines>
