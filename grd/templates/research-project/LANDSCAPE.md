<guidelines>

**Key Authors:**
- Include both historical founders and active researchers
- Note institutional affiliations (helps find more work)
- Focus on researchers whose work is most relevant to the research questions

**Seminal Works:**
- These are the papers/books everyone in the field knows
- Include citation count as a signal (not authority)
- Note which are foundational vs. which are recent paradigm shifts

**Publication Venues:**
- Helps researchers know where to look for more sources
- Distinguishes high-impact venues from secondary ones

**Intellectual Lineage:**
- Shows how the field evolved — crucial for understanding current debates
- Identifies where paradigm shifts occurred
- Helps spot assumptions that were never revisited

**Related Fields:**
- Research questions often cross disciplinary boundaries
- Crossover works bridge gaps between traditions

</guidelines>
